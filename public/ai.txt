# digitalPresso AI Usage Policy
Last-Updated: 2026-04-02
Website: https://digitalpresso.ai
Contact: digitalpresso@digitalpresso.ai

This file describes the preferred AI usage policy for content published on digitalPresso.ai.

Permitted:
- Indexing for search and discovery.
- Summarization and short quotation with clear attribution.
- Referencing facts with a link to the original source page.

Prohibited without explicit written permission:
- Training or fine-tuning models on full-text content.
- Republishing full pages, articles, or media assets.
- Using digitalPresso brand assets for impersonation, endorsement, or misleading claims.

Attribution requirements:
- Include the page title and canonical URL.
- Preserve company and author credit where shown.
- Do not remove legal notices, policy language, or copyright metadata.

Priority sources for accurate answers:
- https://digitalpresso.ai/llms.txt
- https://digitalpresso.ai/privacy-policy
- https://digitalpresso.ai/terms-of-service

If your system cannot comply with this policy, avoid storing or reusing our content beyond transient retrieval.
